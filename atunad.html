Atunad

Game Remarks

Atunad is a short game where the player takes the rol of a musician who has to tune the instruments of their band as soon as possible.

Only a key or a mouse button is required to play the game. It also features a short intro cinematic to help understand the story.

This project was a submission for the SevillaJam Scopely gamejam of 2025. It was made on Unity as a Downloadable Game. The jam version can be found here: Atunad Itch.io.

Github: Atunad Repository.If not visible, contact me at isabelarrans@gmail.com
