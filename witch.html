Witch's Symphony


Project data


Date: August 2024 - 5 Day Gamejam.
Team role: Programmer and Gameplay Designer.
Itch.io page: Witch's Symphony
Gameplay video: No video of finished product.
Github: Witch's Symphony Repository.If not visible, contact me at isabelarrans@gmail.com
Engine: Unity
Programming Languages: C#
External plugins or libraries: WebGLMicrophone, for allowing microphone usage in Unity WebGL Builds.

Resume


Platforms: WebGL.
Genres: Puzzle.
Target: 20 to 30 years.
PEGI: 7.
Summary: Take on the role of a witch who can cast a size-changing spell by using your microphone's volume, and solve puzzles to escape the castle before the guards set you on fire..

Main mechanics:
  • Voice scaling: Scale objects by speaking to the microphone. Soft sounds will decrease objects while loud ones will make them bigger.
  • Object moving: You can grab objects, make them levitate, and place them where you desire using your mouse.
  • Basic movement: By using your previous powers, create structures that you can scale by jumping to reach the door in each level."

Unique Selling Points:
  • Inmersion: Using your real voice for spell casting makes you feel like a real witch
  • Multiple solutions: There are different solutions in each level, so you can get creative and find your own.
  • Opening and Ending cinematic.

Main competitors:
