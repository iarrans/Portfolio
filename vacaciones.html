Vacaciones en familia

Game Remarks

Vacaciones en Familia is a game where you play as a child going on holiday with their parents, seemingly ignoring their conversation while playing a bubble game.

The project was submitted for the GlobalGameJam2025. (48h gamejam) It was made on Unity as a Web Game and it has comptibility with smartphones. The jam version can be found here: Vacaciones en Familia Itch.io.

Github: Vacaciones en familia Repository.If not visible, contact me at isabelarrans@gmail.com

Gameplay features and Screenshots

Main mechanic:
  • Avoid the walkers: People are traversing the area. Touching them will cause damage. Avoid them.
  • Rotating controls: Remember how the controls have rotated so you actually know where to go.
