Scrum RPG

Game Remarks


ScrumRPG is a gamified version of the Scrum Management Framework as a Turn-Based RPG, designed to teach its main steps and theory as if they were phases in a battle against evil tasks. Scrum is a methodology typically used in groups. With this game, users can practice on their own without relying on other teammates. This game was created as a thesis project for the Software Engineering Degree at University of Seville.

Recognitions and awards: Calification received: Distinction. The project also received the National Sistedes Award to Best Software Engineering Degree Thesis Work (Course 2022-2023) and it's currently being used in the University of Seville to aid the teachers when teaching Scrum to the students.

Main features and technologies

Game Engine: Unity, C# as main programming language.
Platforms: Windows, Android.
Itch.io page: ScrumRPG
Github: ScrumRPG Repository. If not visible, contact me at isabelarrans@gmail.com

Main mechanics:
  • Task management/Sprint planning: Decide how many enemies (tasks) and how much strength (story points) the team of developers will battle against. Also, choose the sprint duration and your team roles based on your tasks choices.
  • Turn planification/Daily meeting: Decide on actions to battle (or finish) tasks. The strength of attacks depends on the developer's motivation and mood that day. Each developer can only attack one task within their role. For some battle functions, some features of the ORK Framework V2 plugin were used.
  • Battle stats/Sprint Review: Depending on how many of the originally planned story points were completed, your characters' motivation will increase or decrease.
  • Side Quest/Sprint Retrospective: Choose secondary missions to gain extra motivation at the end of the next battle.
