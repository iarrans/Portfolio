Hotel Calvario

Game Remarks

Hotel Calvario is a point-and-click game where you play as an investigator who must find the culprit.

This game received mentions for Best Art and Best Sound.

The project was submitted for the third edition of SevillaJam. (48 hour gamejam) It was made on Unity as a Web Game. The jam version can be found here: Hotel Calvario Itch.io.

Github: Hotel Calvario Repository.If not visible, contact me at isabelarrans@gmail.com

Gameplay features and Screenshots

Main mechanic:
  • Discover proofs: Navigate through cameras and click on suspicious areas to reveal clues with information. Hours of discovery are important.
  • Time limit: You have a time limit to solve the case. Think before time runs out!
