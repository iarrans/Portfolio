Nanabozho y los dientes

Game Remarks

Nanabozho y los dientes is a survival videogame where player has to protect roots of their teeth by killing enemies before they arrive.

The game can be played both single player and multiplayer up to 6 players in the same keyboard. It has an endless mode with 1 minute levels that get progressively harder.

After the gamejam, the game was updated for allowing playing the endless mode in an online version.

This project was a submission for the GGJ23 (48 hours jam), participated as an artist and game designer. It was made on Unity and built for Windows. The game can be downloaded here Nanabozho y los dientes Itch.io

A reinterpretation of the game was created without the online mode and adding a team competition. The reinterpretation can be downloaded here Fuera de mi Fuero Itch.io

Gameplay features and Screenshots

Main mechanics:
  • Kill the buffalos: Buffalos are the representation of the virus. Kill them by touching them. Warning: some of them may teleport you after you kill them.
  • Nanabozho invocation: When the energy bar is ready, you can call for rabbit god's help, who will randomly move throught the screen killing every enemy it touches.
