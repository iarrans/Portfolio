About me

Thanks for visiting my portfolio. I am Isabel and I would like to share with you my love for videogame creation.

I have always loved bringing joy to people through art and later found passion in programming. I have aimed my efforts towards entering the videogame industry professionally because I believe that videogames blend both of my passions. To achieve this, after my education, I explored the videogame development community through several gamejams and organising game developer networking events being part of DevContact.

One good example of my work could be project Novatuno, a game that reflects my interest to portray different real life events in a playable way. As a player I love to feel related to different game elements and feeling a sense of familiarity through them, which this game aims to achieve.
